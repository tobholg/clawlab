# Code of Conduct

ClawLab expects contributors to act with professionalism and respect.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreement
- Assume good intent and focus on the work
- Give actionable, specific feedback
- Keep discussion welcoming to new contributors

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or sustained hostility
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when behavior undermines a safe and productive environment.
